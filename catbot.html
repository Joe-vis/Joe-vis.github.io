About

Catbot is a bot for discord that I made with one of my friends. It is made with discord.js and node.js.
Catbot started as a simple meme bot that joined voice calls and played cat sounds but has now evolved into a fully working bot with many features.
Catbot is currently hosted on heroku and there is no continuation plan for it after 28th of November 2022 due to heroku closing their free dyno plan.
